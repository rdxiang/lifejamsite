Highlights

LifeJam uses information about your moods, activities and to choose the perfect music. (Okay, maybe not perfect, but we like it.)

Jawbone UP

This lets us play a soothing lullaby when you're going to bed, or a peppy song when you're absolutely estatic.

LifeJams

Queue up your favorite songs for different times of the day. Simply start them with your jawbone, and you've got your own background music.

Responsive

LifeJam takes in account data like weather and your mood when playing songs.
